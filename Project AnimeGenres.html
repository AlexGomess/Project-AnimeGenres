Sports

What's better than an exciting sportsss match? Sports animes are great and it's really fun to see the journey of our protagonists trying to achieve their dreams.

My recommendation for this genre is the anime Haikyu, an exciting volleyball anime!







Supernatural

Do you like ghosts? Vampires? Strange creatures? If the answer is yes, then supernatural is the right genre for you.

My recommendation for this genre is the anime Bakemonogatari, a bunch of tales about ghosts and lots of interesting dialogue!







Shoujo

Sometimes we need some relaxing and wholesome show to remind us of the teenage years we haven't had (haha). Fall in love with the characters and their relationships.

My recommendation for this genre is the anime Kaichou wa maid-sama, the story of the secret maid and the popular handsome boy!







Sci-Fi

If you like technology, machines or time travel, then you'll love this genre. It's amazing to think about the future possibilities of our world.

My recommendation for this genre is the anime Steins;Gate, an intriguing and full of plot twists time travel anime!
